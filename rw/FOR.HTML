Gukuba dukoresheje Umwizenguruko FOR/NEXT(Loop FOR/NEXT) - IchigoJam

Ushobora kubara kuva kuri rimwe kugera kumubare uri muri A wifashishije amabwiriza ya FOR/NEXT. Ubona inyenyeri '*' eshantu(5).
Bihindure noneho ukoreshe imibare wowe ushaka, ukoresheje ibwiriza rya "INPUT".
Andika umubare uwo ariwo wose hanyuma ukande buto yanditseho ENTER!
Ongera wandike undi umubare hanyuma itangire gukora umwizenguruko(Loop)
Ushobora no gukuba imibare!
Shyira umubare mu bubiko C uwo mubare ube '*'.
Kubera ko C=C+A ushobora kubonako igisubizo kirangana na A x '*' kuri buri muzenguruko. Noneho reka C=C+1 ibi nabyo ntakibazo. Niba c=0 cyangwa ngo ureke C,0, C ijye yiyongera muburyo bukomeza.
Gerageza gukora ibi bikurikira!
1. Sha igisubizo cya 7 gukuba 8
2. 8 gukuba 7 bingana bite?
3. Hindura noneho ikimenyetso(Mark) ukoreshe iyo ushaka
4. Noneho hindura mark(ikimenyetso) ige ku nyuguti ebyiri(2)
5. Noneho reba ibyinjira bingana na zeru(0)
