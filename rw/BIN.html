Kora imibare ya Zeru(0) na Rimwe(1) / BIN$

Habaho imibare, inyuguti, ingombajwi, utwitso n'ibimenyetso; Ariko mudasobwa y'umva "Zeru(0)" na "Rimwe(1)" gusa.

Ubusanzwe iyo dufashe 1 tugateranyaho 9 bingana ni 10 aha tuba dufite imibare ibiri. Ariko iyo turi mu mibare ya "1" na "0" gusa, iyo dufashe 1 tugateranyaho 1 bingana ni 10 hanyuma tukaba dufite imibarwa ibiri(2). Iyi niyo mibare ya Zeru(0) na Rimwe(1).

Itegeko twifashisha muri porogaramu kugirango duhindure imibare isanzwe tuzi muri Zeru(0) na Rimwe(1) ni "BIN$".

Ikimenyetso "` " (Dore uko bakandika muri porogaramu: Ukanda Buto yazanditseho "shift" + "@" cyangwa ugakanda Kuri buto yegereye "rimwe(1)" ibumoso bwawe kucyandikishwa(keyboard) yanditseho "`"); Ubwo ni bwo buryo wakwifashisha ukura imibare muri Zeru(0) na Rimwe(1) uyishyira mu mibare dusanzwe tumenyereye.
0,1,2,3,4, ... Bishyire mu mibaee ya zeru na rimwe.
Mu Mibare ya Zeru(0) na Rimwe(1) tubara "imibarwa".
Ni ukuvuga ngo Gatatu(3) Ni imibarwa ibiri(2) ariyo "11" , Naho 8 ikaba imibarwa ine(4) ariyo "10000".
Gerageza gukora uyu mwitozo!
