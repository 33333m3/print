Genzura igihe / WAIT

Reka dukore itegeko ryo "Gutegereza=(wait)" muri IchigoJam.
Agakare kumweru kamyasa muri sikirine(insakazamashusho) gasobanurako keyboard yiteguye gukoreshwa.
Ese waba warigeze kubura ako gakare kaburaho igihe gitoya? Reka twongere tubigerageze.
Iyo uhinguye umubare uri imbere ya "WAIT(gutegereza)", igihe cyo gutegereza kirahinduka.

Agakare uzakubura igihe kinini ugereranyije na mbere. igihe twagenye cyokubura kagakare ni kinini ugereranyije nicya mbere.
Atsa itara kandi unarizimye muburyo bwikoresha.
Use the colon ':'(easy to mistake semicolon ';') to concatenate commands.
"LED1" to light a LED, "WAIT120" to wait 2 seconds, "LED0" to turn off a LED. Light with "LED1", wait with "WAIT120", turn off with"LED0".
It lights the LED a bit.
If you change the number for "WAIT", changes the light pattern of the LED.
The LED lights two times..
You can connect more and more with the colon ":".
Try it!
1. Let's do "WAIT" for 10 seconds
2. Light the LED for 5 seconds and turn it off
3. After typing "WAIT 10000", in the middle, press the ESCAPE key
4. Light a bit after lighting for a long time 5. Try to light the LED with a cool pattern
