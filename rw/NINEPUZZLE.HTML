Umukino w'Udupuzile(Puzzle) icyenda - IchigoJam

Udupizile ni utunyempande 1 kugera 8 kandi dupanze neza.
Subiramo kuva 0 kugera kuri 7, Garagaza uko kwisubiramo kwarangiye.
("%" ka gufasha gushaka ibyasagutse)
Hindura ameza(panel) ukoresheje akerekana aho ugiye kwandika.
("GOSUB" Ni kimwe na "GOTO" ishyirwa aho ushaka gusubira inyuma ukoresheje "RETURN")
Reka ikore kugera kunshuro 200.
(Bisubiremo ukoresheje "RND" kugira ngo ubone calater!)
Garagaza urubanza niba ari ugusiba cyangwa gusiba igihe!
Gerageza gukora ibi:
1. Hindura imibare
2. Noneho ongeraho ijwi ryo gutangiza umukino
3. Kina ijwi mugihe igiye
4. Kora ku buryo itara rimyasa igihe usibye
5. Noneho ongeraho kubika amanota yabaye menshi no kuyagaragaza
