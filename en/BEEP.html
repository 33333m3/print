Make Sounds / BEEP - IchigoJam

The legs of a sounder put in SOUND(SND) and GND of your IchigoJam.
Did you hear a beep sound?
A high tone.
A low tone.
A long tone.
Let's make a rhythm!
WAIT is the command to wait. A slow rhythm if you put it a big number. GOTO is the command to go back to the line 10 to repeat. [ESC] key to stap.
The rhythm will stop while pressing the button of your IchigoJam.
Let's try!
1. Sounds various sounds!
2. Make a rhythm with fast tempo
3. Add this program "12 BEEP 20:WAIT 30" to change the three-time race
4. Make a four-time race rhythm
5. Chcek the rhythem "GOTO 15" to "GOTO 10" on line 15
6. Make your original rhythm!
