Operate the place / LOCATE

Let's display at specified place!
Use "LOCATE" to specify place.
Press ENTER key, '*' will be displayed around the center.
It means that '*' will display on 16 spaces from the left end, and 12 spaces from the top end.
Let's display at a different place.
Change the numbers in the "LOCATE", the place to be displayed will change.
Actually, you can also use "LC" as an abbreviation of "LOCATE".
Also characters can be displayed on two different places.
Also the calculated answer can be displayed.
When you want to clear for monitor, Press F1 key or "CLS".
Try it!
1. Display a "IchigoJam" on 20 from the left end and on 10 from the top end
2. Display a '@' at two different places that is, on 10 from the left end, on 17 from the top end, and on 25 from the left end, on 18 from the top end
3. Display a result of "10-5" on 15 from the left end and 24 from the top end
4. Display a favorite character at favorite place
5. Display a favorite calculation result at favorite place
