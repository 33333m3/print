The legs of a sounder put in SOUND(SND) and GND of your IchigoJam.
Did you hear the sounds Do-re-mi?
Do-re-mi-fa-so-la-ti are CDEFGAB
The octave (=height of soound) can be changed by the "O" and the number.
You can change the length of tone the tone alphabet with the number.
"2" of "CDE2" means a two-part note. Default setting is quartet note. "8" means eighth note that tone length is a half of quartet note.
"#" to raise the tone a semitone, "-" to lower the tone a semitone.
"R" means silence. You can change the length of tones using the nunmber sunch this "R2" alse.
"T" means to set up the tempo. Default setting "T120" means 120 quartet note per second.
