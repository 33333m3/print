Janken Game

Janken is Japanese fighting game using them hand.
- Gu: Sign your hand like a rock, stand no fingers. (Gu wins Choki)
- Choki: Sign your hand like a scissors, stand 2 fingers. (Choki wins Pa)
- Pa: Sign your hand like a paper, stand 5 fingers. (Pa wins Gu)

Use randam command with number 3 if you want to decide Gu, Choki or Pa by your computer.
For instance 0 means Gu, 1 means Choki, 2 means Pa.
Try to type RUN or press F5 key repeatly!
Press F5 key and say your hand to fight after your call "Jan ken"!
