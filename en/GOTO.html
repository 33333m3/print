Make a night signal / GOTO

A blinking signal for the night,it turns on and off every 0.5 seconds.
Typr line 10, and return, and change the head "10" to "20", and press ENTER key.
Confirm the line 10 copied to line 20 with "LIST"!
About 30,000 times until the morning. Blink all the time with "GOTO".
The number at behind of "GOTO" is jumping destination.
> line 10, blink one time
> line 20, jump to line 10
When it stops, press [ESC] key.
Fast blink
Display a program with "LIST"(=F4 key).
Change two "30"s in the line 10 to "3", press ENTER key, and "RUN"(=F5 key).
Make a line 15 between line 10 and line 20.
Repeat alternately between short blink and long blink.
